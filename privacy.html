Privacy Policy

VPN Connect is built around one principle — your activity is your business, not ours.

Last updated: May 2025
🔒

THE SHORT VERSION VPN Connect does not collect, store, or sell your personal information. No account is required. We do not log your browsing activity, connection times, or IP addresses. The VPN connection data needed to route your traffic is used in real time only and is never stored.

01

Information We Collect

VPN Connect does not collect personally identifiable information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any information that identifies you as an individual.

No account creation or sign-in is required to use the app. You install it and use it — that's all.

02

How the VPN Works

When you connect, the app establishes an encrypted tunnel between your device and a VPN server. Your internet traffic is routed through this tunnel, masking your IP address and encrypting your data in transit.

The cryptographic keys and server configuration used to establish this connection are used only at the moment of connection. They are not transmitted to any analytics service, database, or third party.

The app may retrieve a configuration from our server at launch — this configuration contains server details and operational settings. It does not include any personal information and is not used to identify or track you.

03

Permissions We Request

VPN — Android requires explicit user approval before any VPN connection can be established. This permission is requested when you first tap Connect and is required to create the encrypted tunnel.

Internet — Required to connect your device to the VPN server and to allow the VPN to route your traffic.

Network State — Used to detect whether your device has an internet connection, so the app can show the correct status.

Foreground Service — Required by Android for the VPN to remain active while running in the background. This keeps your connection alive when you switch to other apps.

You can revoke VPN access at any time through Android Settings. Doing so will immediately disconnect any active VPN session.

04

Remote Configuration

The app fetches a configuration file from our servers when it launches. This configuration includes the list of available VPN servers, a minimum required app version, and optional maintenance status.

This request does not transmit any personal data. The server receives a standard HTTP request from your IP address — the same as any web page load — and responds with the configuration. We do not store or log these requests beyond what is needed to keep the server operational.

If the configuration cannot be fetched — for example, due to a network error — the app falls back to built-in defaults and continues to function normally.

05

No Activity Logging

VPN Connect does not log your browsing activity, connection history, session durations, or the websites and services you access through the VPN.

We do not store the originating IP address of your device, the destination IP addresses of your traffic, or any DNS queries made through the tunnel.

We have no way to correlate your real identity with any activity performed while connected — by design.

06

Third-Party Services

VPN Connect does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or social login providers.

The app uses the following third-party libraries on the device for core functionality: networking, connectivity detection, and VPN tunnel management. These libraries operate locally on your device and do not transmit data to external parties on our behalf.

If you open external links from within the app — such as a support page or the Play Store listing — those destinations are governed by their own privacy policies.

07

Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to retain or delete on our end.

Any app preferences stored on your device (such as last-selected server) are kept locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

08

Children's Privacy

VPN Connect is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from any user, including children. Since no personal data is collected, the app presents no specific data privacy risk to younger users.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
